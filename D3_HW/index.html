Heart Risk Analysis

What are the most dangerous health risks the United States deals with?

One of the biggest health risks in the United States is Heart Attacks. The data showed that if you are someone who holds a bachelor degree you have a better chance of not having a heart attack. This directly shows that the more educated citizens are the more knowledge they have on health. The data also showed that people who make anywhere above 50,000 + a year have a higher chance of avoiding heart attacks than individuals who earn 50,000 or less per year. The Data also showed that people who drink more are more likely to have a heart attack vs those who do not drink.
